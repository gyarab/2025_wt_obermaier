Welcome to AOIC

What is AOIC

At AOIC, we believe every child deserves the freedom to make their own decisions — whether it’s eating ice cream for breakfast, building a pillow fort instead of doing homework, or accidentally setting off the school fire alarm (again).

Founded by people who thought they understood kids but really just forgot how to be adults, AOIC proudly stands as the world’s most questionably organized association for young independence. Our mission? To promote responsibility, individuality, and the occasional chaos that comes with both.

We offer programs like “Self-Guided Naptime,” “Budgeting with Monopoly Money,” and “Conflict Resolution through Rock-Paper-Scissors.” Sure, our methods might not be perfect (or legal in three countries), but we’re trying our best — kind of.

Join AOIC today — where independence meets mild confusion, and every day is a new opportunity to learn absolutely nothing useful.
